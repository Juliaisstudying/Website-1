About Us

TDX Driving Academy is a team of professional trainers, licensed Instructors, and talented coaches who are highly experienced in Commercial Driving Education. Our entire team is focused on providing our students with, not only mandatory knowledge for the test, but with practical driving skills that Commercial Drivers require. We share first hand real experience of Driving with our students that makes entire course fun easy and exciting!

TDX Driving Academy is a member of the TDX Group which has long history of commercial driving as a transportation company with millions of miles traveled across North America. The valuable experience that our instructors have obtained within TDX Logistics will be generosity shared with our student. I can tell you, there are lots of funny and helpful stories waiting for you!
